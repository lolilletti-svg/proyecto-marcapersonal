## Versión completa (English)

People keep asking me how I built the reconciliation app without being a developer.

The honest answer: the hard part wasn't the code.

It was knowing what to build.

When you work in AP long enough, you accumulate something valuable without realizing it:
a detailed mental model of how money moves, where it gets stuck, and why the numbers don't match.

That mental model is what tells you:
- The vendor number format needs to be normalized before you match anything
- One vendor can appear under 4 different location names in the same sheet
- A credit on one statement doesn't cancel a debt on another — they need to be reconciled separately

You can't ask AI to figure that out for you.
You have to know it first, then translate it into instructions.

That's the skill AP builds that no one talks about:
the ability to decompose a messy, exception-heavy process into clean logic.

Turns out that's also exactly what automation requires.
