## Versión completa (English)

Every Monday at 8 AM, a script sends statement request emails to every vendor in my AP portfolio.

I don't open Gmail. I don't draft anything. I don't remember to do it.

It just happens.

The script reads a Google Sheet with vendor names, owners, and emails.
It filters to my portfolio (vendors assigned to me).
It sends a personalized email to each one requesting their updated statement.
When they reply with an attachment, a second script saves it automatically to the right Drive folder.

By Monday afternoon, I already have responses coming in — without having done anything.

What changed:
- No more "I forgot to email Imperial on Thursday"
- No more hunting for the statement in my inbox on Tuesday
- No more wondering which vendors are still outstanding

The process still requires judgment — reviewing what comes in, chasing the ones who don't respond.
But the initiation is no longer something I carry in my head.
