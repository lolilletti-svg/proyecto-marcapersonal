# Editorial checklist

- Is the post based on something real?
- Does it position Lucía toward process automation and improvement?
- Is the angle sharper than the raw anecdote?
- Is the lesson transferable?
- Is the tone clear and credible?
- Are clichés removed?
- Is there an actual reason for someone to care?
- Is the hook specific enough?
- Does this help the profile, not just the feed?
