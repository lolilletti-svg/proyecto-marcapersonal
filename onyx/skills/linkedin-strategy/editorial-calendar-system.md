# editorial calendar system

Goal:
Turn strategy into a realistic publishing system.

Use this skill to:
- create a 2 to 6 week calendar
- balance content pillars
- assign themes to dates or windows
- make sure each slot serves a positioning goal
- avoid overposting weak or repetitive content

Checks:
- every week should mix proof, reflection, and evolution
- no slot should exist without a clear source of truth
- keep room for timely ideas and experiments
